# Knowledge Graph Strategy

## Node Types

- **Project:** an outcome with constraints and a finish condition.
- **Knowledge:** one durable concept in your own words.
- **Research paper:** evidence, method, limitations, and relevance.
- **Experiment:** hypothesis → configuration → result → decision.
- **Decision:** context, options, choice, consequence.
- **Person/meeting:** commitments and decisions, not transcripts.

## Linking Rules

1. Every knowledge note links upward to a roadmap or domain map.
2. Every experiment links to one project and the concepts it tests.
3. Every paper links to at least one knowledge note or project question.
4. Every meeting links to its project and records decisions/tasks.
5. Prefer meaningful links in sentences over a generic “related” dump.

## Retrieval Layers

- Dashboards answer “what needs attention?”
- Maps of content answer “what do I know?”
- Backlinks answer “where is this used?”
- Search answers “where did I mention this?”
- Graph view is for local exploration, not primary navigation.
